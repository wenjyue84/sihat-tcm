## Myth 1: It Hurts
Most patients describe the sensation as a dull ache or a "heavy" feeling, known as *De Qi*. It is rarely painful like a vaccine injection.

## Myth 2: It is Just Placebo
Numerous studies show acupuncture stimulates the release of endorphins and affects the nervous system in measurable ways.

## Myth 3: It is Religious
Acupuncture is a medical practice based on physiology and meridian theory, not a religious belief system.
