## Ginger Tea (Sheng Jiang)
Best for: **Cold constitutions**, stomach aches, and nausea. It warms the stomach and dispels cold.

## Red Date Tea (Hong Zao)
Best for: **Building blood** and energy. Great for post-menstrual recovery or fatigue.

## Chrysanthemum Tea (Ju Hua)
Best for: **Heatiness**, sore throat, and red eyes. It is cooling and calms the Liver.
