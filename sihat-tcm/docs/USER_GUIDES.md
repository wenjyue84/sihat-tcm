## Diagnosis Workflow Guide

### The Complete TCM Examination Process

#### Step 1: Basic Information (2 minutes)

**What to prepare**:

- Your current height and weight
- Main health concern description
- List of current symptoms

**Tips for accuracy**:

- Be specific about your main concern (e.g., "Chronic fatigue for 3 months" vs "Tired")
- Use the symptom chips for common issues
- Mention symptom duration and severity

#### Step 2: Inquiry Chat (5-10 minutes)

**What the AI will ask about**:

- Detailed symptom patterns
- Sleep quality and patterns
- Appetite and digestion
- Emotional state and stress levels
- Lifestyle habits
- Medical history

**How to give good answers**:

- Be honest and thorough
- Describe how symptoms affect your daily life
- Mention any patterns you've noticed
- Include current medications or treatments

**Example good responses**:

- ❌ "I sleep badly"
- ✅ "I fall asleep easily but wake up at 3 AM every night and can't get back to sleep. This has been happening for 2 months."

#### Step 3: Tongue Examination (2 minutes)

**Best practices for tongue photos**:

**Lighting**:

- Use natural daylight when possible
- Avoid yellow indoor lighting
- Face a window or go outside

**Positioning**:

- Stick tongue out naturally (don't force it)
- Keep tongue flat and relaxed
- Ensure entire tongue is visible
- Keep mouth wide open

**Preparation**:

- Don't eat colored foods 30 minutes before
- Don't brush your tongue beforehand
- Avoid coffee, tea, or colored drinks

**Common mistakes to avoid**:

- ❌ Taking photo in dim lighting
- ❌ Tongue too tense or curled
- ❌ Only showing tip of tongue
- ❌ Recent consumption of colored foods

#### Step 4: Face Examination (1 minute)

**Photo requirements**:

- Front-facing, direct view
- Even lighting on face
- Remove glasses if possible
- Neutral expression
- Clean face (no makeup if possible)

**What the AI analyzes**:

- Overall complexion and color
- Facial zones (forehead=heart, cheeks=liver/lung, etc.)
- Eye brightness and condition
- Lip color and moisture

#### Step 5: Audio Recording (Optional, 1 minute)

**How to record effectively**:

- Find a quiet environment
- Hold phone 6-12 inches from mouth
- Say "Ahhh" for 5-10 seconds
- Describe how you feel in normal voice
- Mention any breathing difficulties

**What to include in your recording**:

- Voice strength demonstration
- Any cough patterns
- Breathing sounds
- Normal speech patterns

#### Step 6: Pulse Measurement (2 minutes)

**Three methods available**:

1. **Tap Method** (Recommended for beginners):
   - Find pulse on wrist or neck
   - Tap the button with each heartbeat
   - Continue for 15-30 seconds
   - System calculates BPM automatically

2. **Manual Entry**:
   - Enter BPM if you know it from a device
   - Include pulse quality if you can identify it

3. **Camera Method** (Android Chrome only):
   - Place fingertip over camera lens and flash
   - Hold very still for 10 seconds
   - Works best in dim lighting

**Pulse quality guide** (for advanced users):

- **Floating (浮)**: Easy to feel with light pressure
- **Deep (沉)**: Requires firm pressure to feel
- **Rapid (数)**: Faster than normal (>90 BPM)
- **Slow (迟)**: Slower than normal (<60 BPM)
- **Slippery (滑)**: Smooth, flowing sensation
- **Wiry (弦)**: Tight, like a guitar string

#### Step 7: Smart Connect (Optional, 3 minutes)

**Supported devices and apps**:

- Apple Health (iPhone)
- Google Fit (Android)
- Samsung Health
- Fitbit devices
- Garmin devices
- Manual entry

**Useful data to connect**:

- Daily step count
- Sleep duration and quality
- Average heart rate
- Blood pressure readings
- Weight measurements

#### Step 8: Review & Generate Report (1 minute)

**Before generating**:

- Review all entered information for accuracy
- Customize report options:
  - Include/exclude demographics
  - Include/exclude medical history
  - Select TCM recommendation types
- Choose report language

**Report generation time**: 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on complexity

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login and Account Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in to account
**Solutions**:

1. Check email and password spelling
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check email spam folder for verification
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser or incognito mode

**Problem**: Email verification not received
**Solutions**:

1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add platform domain to email whitelist
3. Request new verification email
4. Contact support if issue persists

#### Diagnosis Issues

**Problem**: Camera not working during examination
**Solutions**:

1. Allow camera permissions in browser
2. Check if other apps are using camera
3. Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
4. Use "Upload Image" alternative
5. Restart browser or device

**Problem**: Audio recording fails
**Solutions**:

1. Allow microphone permissions
2. Check microphone is connected and working
3. Test microphone in other applications
4. Try different browser
5. Skip audio step if necessary (it's optional)

**Problem**: Report generation takes too long or fails
**Solutions**:

1. Check internet connection stability
2. Don't refresh page during generation
3. Wait up to 3 minutes for completion
4. Try generating report again
5. Contact support if repeatedly fails

#### Mobile App Issues

**Problem**: App crashes or won't start
**Solutions**:

1. Force close and restart app
2. Restart device
3. Update app to latest version
4. Clear app cache and data
5. Reinstall app if necessary

**Problem**: Sync issues between devices
**Solutions**:

1. Check internet connection on both devices
2. Log out and log back in
3. Force sync in settings
4. Wait a few minutes for automatic sync
5. Contact support for data recovery

#### Feature-Specific Issues

**Problem**: Meal Planner won't generate plans
**Solutions**:

1. Ensure you have completed at least one diagnosis
2. Check dietary preferences are set correctly
3. Try clearing preferences and setting again
4. Check internet connection
5. Try generating plan at different time

**Problem**: Snore Analysis not working
**Solutions**:

1. Check audio file format is supported
2. Ensure recording is at least 30 minutes
3. Check file size isn't too large
4. Try recording in quieter environment
5. Use different device for recording

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Sihat TCM free to use?**
A: Basic diagnosis features are free. Premium features like AI Meal Planner and advanced analytics may require subscription.

**Q: How accurate is the AI diagnosis?**
A: The AI is trained on extensive TCM knowledge and provides consistent analysis. However, it should complement, not replace, professional medical care.

**Q: Can I use this for serious medical conditions?**
A: Sihat TCM is for wellness and preventive care. For serious conditions, always consult licensed healthcare professionals.

**Q: Is my health data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow strict privacy policies and healthcare data protection standards.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Chrome is recommended for best experience.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: The mobile app has limited offline functionality. Full features require internet connection for AI processing.

**Q: How long are my reports saved?**
A: Reports are saved indefinitely for registered users. Guest mode reports are available for 30 days.

### TCM-Specific Questions

**Q: What if I don't understand my TCM diagnosis?**
A: Use the "Report Q&A" feature to ask questions about your diagnosis. You can also consult with a licensed TCM practitioner.

**Q: How often should I get a diagnosis?**
A: For general wellness, every 2-4 weeks. For specific conditions, follow recommendations in your report or consult a practitioner.

**Q: Can I follow the herbal recommendations without consulting a doctor?**
A: Herbal formulas should only be taken under guidance of a licensed TCM practitioner. Dietary and lifestyle recommendations are generally safe to follow.
